Dot Product

Multiplies matching components and sums them, representing the projection of one vector onto another.

Cosine Similarity

The cosine of the angle between vectors, showing how aligned they are.

Pearson Correlation

Centers each vector and computes the cosine of the angle between them.

OLS Coefficient

The slope of the best-fit line predicting one vector from the other.
